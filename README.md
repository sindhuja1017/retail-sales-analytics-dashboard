# 📊 Retail Sales Analytics Dashboard

> Interactive Retail Sales Analytics Dashboard built using **Power BI** to analyze sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, and product insights through dynamic visualizations.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project demonstrates the development of an interactive Retail Sales Analytics Dashboard using Microsoft Power BI. The dashboard transforms raw retail sales data into meaningful business insights, enabling users to monitor KPIs, identify sales trends, evaluate product performance, and analyze customer segments.

The report is designed for business users who need quick, data-driven insights to support operational and strategic decision-making.

## 💡 Key Business Insights

- Electronics generated the highest profit contribution.
- North region achieved the highest overall sales.
- Premium customers contributed the largest share of revenue.
- Laptop was the highest-selling product.
- Credit Card was the most frequently used payment method.
- Monthly sales peaked during March before declining in April.
- Profit margins varied across product brands, highlighting opportunities for pricing optimization.
